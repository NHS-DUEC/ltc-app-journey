The contact number in your NHS App account is an international phone number. You need to call 111 using a UK phone number.

When you call 111, tell them you completed an assessment online.

The health adviser will:

  • ask you to repeat your answers
  • transfer you to a nurse if necessary
  • book an appointment for you if you need one
