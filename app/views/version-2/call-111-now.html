You need to speak to a health adviser

We cannot complete your assessment online. You need to speak to a health adviser to get the right help.

Calling 111 from this screen means:

  • you will speak to someone more quickly than dialling 111 from your keypad
  • your personal details (name, postcode, NHS number) will be sent
  • the health adviser will know your main symptom

The health adviser will ask you to confirm details such as your name. You will also have to answer some more questions about your symptoms.

What if I get cut off?

If you lose phone signal you will need to start again. You can call 111 from your keypad but your personal details will not be sent.
