Call 111 to speak to a health adviser now

The contact number in your NHS App account is an international number. You need to call 111 with a UK phone number.

Why can't I do this online?

Complex medical problems, or diagnosed conditions, cannot be dealth with online. You need to speak to a 111 health adviser, who will transfer you to a nurse if necessary.

What happens when I call?

Tell the health adviser we have not been able to finish your assessment online. Youw will be asked similar questions to begin with, but you will get the right help.
